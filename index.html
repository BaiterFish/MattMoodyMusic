Lessons

Instruments

Piano Lessons:
Beginner to advanced students with a teaching styles focused on mastering the fundamentals of reading sheet music, understanding rhythm, and teaching students to play their favorite songs.

Cello Lessons:
Beginner to intermediate students with a teaching emphasis on reading sheet music, developing good intonation, and group playing.

In-Home Lessons


High quality lessons from an experienced teacher right in your own home. Students will have no need to communte. Because of this students are expected to provide their own instrument, books, and other materials.

Rates

~$60/hour of lesson time

The recommended lesson time will vary based on student age, skill level, and desired goals. Typcially weekly for 30-60 minutes.

The rate of lessons could change according to student location and avaliable lesson times.
